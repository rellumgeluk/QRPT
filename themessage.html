The message

Hey there, my name is Nathalia, and I’ve found you.

Lately, QR codes have become a part of our daily lives — we scan them at restaurants, stores, and events without even thinking twice.
But what if I told you that by scanning an unknown QR code, you could be handing over personal data to someone with bad intentions?
The reality is, QR codes can be easily manipulated to redirect you to malicious sites or even steal your information without you knowing.
In a world where it feels almost impossible to truly keep our data private, we have to be more
mindful of what we share and where we share it.
That's why I decided to raise awareness and remind people to think twice before scanning a random QR code.
It's a small step, but it could make a big difference in keeping your data safe.

Thanks for checking it. Stay Safe!
